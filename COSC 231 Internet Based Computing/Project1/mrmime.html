Who is Mr. Mime?

General Information

Evolution:

 All Mr. Mimes begin life as Mime Jr. which is a smaller, baby-like, and very playful version of Mr. Mime. A Mime Jr. can only evolve into a Mr. Mime when he has finally learned the move "mimic," which is one of their signiature moves.

Similar Pokemon:

  • The Abra Evolution Line - They are both very intelligent, psychic, and humanoid.
  • The Machop Evolution Line - While Machops are much rougher and violent, they are also humanoid and more intelligent than the average Pokemon.
  • The Drowzee Evolution Line - They are both very intelligent, psychic, and humanoid.
  • The Meditite Evolution Line - They are both intelligent, psychic, and humanoid. However, the Meditite line is also particularly good at physical fighting, which Mr. Mime isn't.

Powers & Strengths

Powers:

  • Mimic - Mr Mime's Signiature Move - He perfectly copies an enemies attack.
  • Psychic - A common yet very powerful psychic-type move, can also lower his target's defense.
  • Confusion - Deals moderate damage, and can confuse the enemy into damaging themselves.
  • Baton Pass - A non-damaging technique that let's Mr. Mime quickly tag in another Pokemon.

Strength:

 Mr. Mimes strengths are his Special Attack and Special Defence, which allow him to deal a lot and block a lot of non-physical damage. Mr. Mime is capable of dealing a lot of damage, while still providing a lot of utility. He is very tricky and cunning as an opponent.

Weaknesses

Weaknesses:

 Mr. Mime has poor attack and defense, so he is particularly vulnerable to physically attacking pokemon. Furthermore, as a psychic and fairy type, Mr. Mime takes double damage from the pokemon types listed below.

Pokemon Types That Counter Mr. Mime:

  • Poison
  • Steel
  • Ghost

 We suggest trying to confuse these types of Pokemon, or if necessary, escape with baton pass to a Pokemon who is more suited to defeat that particular enemy. Mr. Mime can easily defeat Pokemon that counter him, it just requires experience and finesse.
